The Firm

The Firm of Musyimi & Company was initially established as a purely conveyance practice with corporate clients but has since grown into a four department firm, with each department headed by a partner and backed Associates and Legal Assistants. Over the years, our scope of services has also grown, as has our clients' base, which now includes individuals (local and international), corporate clientele (local and international) and state clients.

Foundation

This Law Firm was founded on principles of integrity and service, and endeavours to uphold at all times in our dealings, the dignity of the human being, justice for both our internal and external clients, service with humility and intergrity.
