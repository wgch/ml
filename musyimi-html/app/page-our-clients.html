Corporate

We have a wide ranging corporate clientele to wit:

  • Financial institutions including banks, insurance companies and microfinance banks. Our mainstay in this regard are locally owned financial institutions and joint ventures between Kenyan citizens and non-citizens.
  • Property companies including developers, architects, contractors and other service providers in the real estate value chain.

  • Various Foreign Missions.

  • Branches of foreign companies established in Kenya under the provision of the Companies Act.
